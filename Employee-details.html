Introduction

At Ecotone Solutions, we excel in delivering practical and cost-effective solutions across Environmental, Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical Engineering sectors. Based in Uttar Pradesh and headquartered in Lucknow, our mission is to provide technically robust, economically viable, and aesthetically appealing solutions, from construction and DPR preparation to environmental studies and surveying, using modern techniques.

Our vision is to be the most innovative and diverse consulting engineering firm in India, setting industry benchmarks for excellence. We prioritize a customer-centric approach, dedicating resources to the development of new products and services while upholding technical excellence and ethical standards. Our specialties include impact assessment, regulatory compliance, and solutions for water conservation and wastewater management.

With a highly qualified team and partnerships with accredited EIA Consultant Organizations and NABL laboratories, we ensure the highest quality standards. Our professionals, each with over 15 years of expertise, conduct comprehensive field visits and surveys to design tailored solutions that meet client needs. At Ecotone Solutions, we continuously innovate, exceeding expectations and delivering sustainable solutions that positively impact the environment and society.
